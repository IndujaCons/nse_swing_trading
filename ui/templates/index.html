Sector Momentum Heatmap i
How to read the heatmap:
Each pill shows 3 lines — Sector Name, RS %, and Momentum Score.
RS % (middle) — Sector's 20-day return minus Nifty's 20-day return. Green = outperforming Nifty, Red = underperforming.
Momentum (bottom) — How fast the RS is changing. ▲▲ positive = sector RS is rising (getting stronger). ▼▼ negative = sector RS is falling (weakening).
Key insight: A sector at -11% RS but ▲▲ +27 momentum means it was deeply weak but is now recovering fast — early reversal catch. This often scores higher than a sector sitting flat at +4%.
Background: Green tint = momentum positive (sector strengthening). Red tint = momentum negative (sector weakening).
Sorted left-to-right by momentum score (strongest rising first). Hover any pill for full 5d/10d/20d delta breakdown.
